Pork Adobo

Quick and Easy Filipino comfort dish

Ingredients

  • 600 g pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • shit ton of garlic
  • lots of black peppercorns
  • 5 Bay leaves
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Cut pork belly into cube sized pieces
  2. Prepare a large dutch oven or a large skillet, drizzle some oil and sear the pork belly until light brown on both sides
  3. Add garlic, and peppercorns. Stir to bloom the spices, and aromatics or until the garlic turns into a light golden color
  4. Add soy sauce and vinegar to simmer
  5. Add bay leaves and pork belly into the mixture, heat on high if it stops simmering, then turn it back down low for it to continue simmering
  6. Simmer for at least an hour
  7. Enjoy with rice
